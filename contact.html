Frequently Asked Questions

How does Estate Sale Connect work?

Families submit their estate sale needs through our form, and qualified estate sale companies in their area can contact them directly to provide quotes and services.

Is this service free for families?

Yes! Our service is completely free for families. Estate sale companies pay to access leads, which allows us to provide this valuable matching service at no cost to families.

How do I become a partner company?

Estate sale companies can request access by contacting us. We verify credentials and insurance before granting access to our lead marketplace.

What areas do you serve?

We connect families and estate sale companies nationwide. Our network is growing daily to serve more communities across the United States.
